# Phase II — EARLY QUALITY

## When
- After Phase I (BASE), when volume is stable
- See timeline by distance below

## Timeline by distance
| Dystans | Kiedy | Czas trwania |
|---------|-------|-------------|
| 5K      | 4–8 weeks before race / virtual test | 2–3 weeks |
| 10K     | 6–10 weeks before race / virtual test | 3–4 weeks |
| HM      | 8–12 weeks before race | 3–4 weeks |
| Maraton | 12–18 weeks before race | 4–6 weeks |

## Goal by distance

### 5K — Speed focus
Introduce R-pace (repetitions) and short hills. Legs need to feel fast before Phase III intervals.
- **Quality accent options:**
  - 10–16× 200m @ R-pace, full rest (90–120 sec walk)
  - Strides + Hills: easy + 8×100m strides + 6× 45 sec hills
  - Fast striders: 6× 150m @ mile pace, full rest

### 10K — Economy + first tempo exposure
Mix of R-pace and early M-pace. Introduce the idea of sustained effort.
- **Quality accent options:**
  - 8–12× 200–400m @ R-pace, full rest
  - Strides + Hills: easy + 6×100m + 6× 60 sec hills
  - Easy Tempo: 15–20 min @ M-pace (NOT T-pace yet)

### HM — Economy + M-pace
Focus on R-pace economy and introducing M-pace inserts in long runs.
- **Quality accent options:**
  - Repetitions: 8–12× 200–400m @ R-pace, full rest
  - Strides + Hills: easy + 6×100m + 6× hills 60 sec
  - Easy Tempo: 20–25 min @ M-pace

### Maraton — M-pace foundation
Build comfort at M-pace. Long runs with M-pace inserts become the key workout.
- **Quality accent options:**
  - Long run with M-pace inserts (e.g. 16km with 6km @ M-pace in middle)
  - Easy Tempo: 30 min @ M-pace
  - Strides: easy 60 min + 8×100m strides

## Daniels' volume rules
- Max R per session: **5% of weekly volume**
- Long run: max 25–30% of volume or 2.5h

## Avoid
- Full T-pace or I-pace throughout the phase (save for Phase III)
- For 5K/10K: races longer than the goal distance
- For HM/M: races longer than 10K

## Rotation
- Repetitions ↔ Hills week by week
- Every 3–4 weeks: recovery week

## Progress markers
- R-pace feels increasingly "lighter"
- Cadence at easy pace naturally rises (effect of strides)
- No DOMS after R sessions
