# Phase II — EARLY QUALITY

## When
- 8–12 weeks before the race
- After Phase BASE, when volume is stable

## Goal
Introduce speed and running economy (R-pace). Prepare legs for later Phase III work (I/T).

## Weekly structure
- **2× Easy run**
- **1× Long run** (with optional M-pace inserts in second half of phase)
- **1× Quality accent — rotation:**
  - Repetitions: 8–12× 200–400m @ R-pace, full rest
  - Strides + Hills: easy + 6×100m + 6× hills 60sec
  - Easy Tempo: 15–20 min @ M-pace (NOT T-pace yet!)

## Daniels' volume rules
- Max R per session: **5% of weekly volume**
- Long run: max 25–30% of volume or 2.5h

## Avoid
- Full T-pace or I-pace throughout the phase (save for Phase III)
- Races longer than 5km

## Rotation
- Repetitions ↔ Hills week by week
- Every 4 weeks: recovery week

## Progress markers
- R-pace feels increasingly "lighter"
- Cadence at easy pace naturally rises (effect of strides)
- No DOMS after R sessions

## Typical duration
3–4 weeks before transitioning to Phase III
